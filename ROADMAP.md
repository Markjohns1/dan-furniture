## Phase 2: AI-Powered Features ðŸ¤–

### AI Product Description Generator
> **Estimated Development: 2-3 weeks**

Automatically generate professional product descriptions from uploaded images.

**How it works:**
1. Admin uploads product photo
2. AI analyzes the image
3. System auto-generates:
   - Suggested product name
   - Professional description (50-150 words)
   - Suggested category
   - Detected colors/materials
   - SEO-optimized keywords

**Benefits:**
- Save 5-10 minutes per product listing
- Consistent, professional descriptions
- SEO-friendly content
- No copywriting skills needed

**Technology:** Open-source AI models (LLaVA, BLIP-2, Florence-2)
